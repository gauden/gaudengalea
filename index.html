“Only Connect”

The tagline, “Only Connect”, a fragment from E. M. Forster, provides a motto for this personal site, which serves as a sandbox and a hub, connecting my public health practice to extracurricular fields of interest.

I am a public health physician, operating at national and global levels since the mid-1980s. I am also deeply interested in computer programming and the applications of data science to the practice of public health today. This site is intended to fuse the two interests, a place to explore and demonstrate the connections between the two areas.
